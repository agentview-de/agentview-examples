# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Everyone interacting in this project — issues, pull requests,
discussions — is expected to keep it welcoming and harassment-free,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity. This repo
exists to help people learn by example; assume good faith and be kind
to beginners.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive. Critique code, not people.
- Assume the other person is trying to help.
- Accept that this is a small, volunteer-maintained reference repo:
  responses may take time, and not every request will be accepted.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discriminatory or sexual language, personal attacks,
trolling, or publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to **conduct@agentview.de**. Reports are
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove comments, reject
contributions, or block accounts that violate this code.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), kept
deliberately short for a small project.
